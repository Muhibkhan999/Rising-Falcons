About us

Welcome to Rising Falcons Team - where passion meets excellence.
We are dedicated to nurturing cricket talent and building champions.

  • Mission
  • Vision
  • Values

Our mission is to promote cricket excellence through dedicated training, fostering talent, and building a strong community of cricket enthusiasts. We strive to develop players with exceptional skills and sportsmanship.

We aim to make cricket accessible to all and create opportunities for aspiring cricketers to reach their full potential through professional coaching and guidance.

By organizing tournaments, training camps and other cricket activities, we work to elevate the standard of cricket while instilling values of teamwork, discipline and fair play.

To become the leading cricket academy that transforms passionate players into professional cricketers through world-class training and development programs.

We envision creating a cricket ecosystem that nurtures talent from grassroots to professional levels while promoting the spirit of the game.

Our goal is to be recognized globally as a center of cricket excellence that produces skilled players and contributes to the growth of cricket.

Through innovation in training methods and a holistic approach to player development, we aim to set new standards in cricket coaching and player management.

We believe in integrity, dedication, teamwork and continuous improvement as the cornerstones of cricket excellence.

Our values include respect for the game, commitment to player development, and fostering a positive competitive spirit.

The Rising Falcons is a pioneering cricket academy that focuses on developing young cricketers into world-class players. We provide a comprehensive training program with expert coaches and trainers to help players improve their skills and reach their full potential.

Our approach is centered on fostering a culture of excellence, sportsmanship and teamwork.

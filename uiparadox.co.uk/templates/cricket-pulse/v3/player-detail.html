Biography

Aaliyan Suhail is one of cricket's most accomplished batsmen and former captain of the Rising Falcons team. Known for his aggressive batting style and exceptional fitness levels, he has broken numerous records across all formats of the game. His dedication to the sport and relentless pursuit of excellence has earned him recognition as one of cricket's modern greats.

Making his international debut in 2008, Aaliyan quickly established himself as a key player in the Indian lineup. His remarkable consistency and ability to perform under pressure have helped Falcons achieve numerous victories. Beyond his batting prowess, his leadership has transformed the team's approach to fitness and professionalism. He continues to inspire millions of cricket enthusiasts worldwide with his passion and commitment to the game.
